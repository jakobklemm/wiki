Socia media

The goal of social media sites is not to connect people or to enhance their lives, the goal is to reduce their users freedom of choice in order to increase their profits. Through data these companies can make choices for you, present them with their choice and still give you the feeling of being in control.

This can quickly lead to a feedback loop, where companies and platforms work together and feed their products directly to customers without using the free market and ignoring the principles of Capitalism.
